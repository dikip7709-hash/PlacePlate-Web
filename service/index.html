Frequently Asked Question

Answers to common questions about templates, downloads, and customization.

How do I start a custom presentation project?

Simply click “Start Your Project” and fill out a short order form. Once we review your request, we’ll guide you through the next steps.

What does the design process look like?

We start by understanding your goals, content, and audience. Then we design your slides using structured storytelling, professional visuals, and animations that support your message.

What is the payment process?

To start the project, a 30% deposit is required. The remaining balance is paid after the final presentation is approved.

How long does a custom presentation project take?

Most custom presentation projects are completed within 4–5 working days, depending on complexity and number of slides.

What will I receive at the end of the project?

You’ll receive a fully editable PowerPoint file, ready to use for presentations, pitches, or submissions.
